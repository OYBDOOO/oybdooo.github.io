About Me

I’m Boding Ouyang, a PhD student in Planetary Science at the School of Earth and Space Sciences, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I work in the USTC Astrobiology Lab under the supervision of Professor Jihua Hao.

My research centers on:

  • Evolution of Planetary Atmospheres
  • Atmospheric Hydrogen Escape Processes
